Our Mission

Our team at Pacific Trails Resort strives to provide an exciting yet serene stay for all guests on the beautiful coast of Big Sur: a goal we've upheld since the resort's establishment. We value your experience as a guest here as much as your do! It is vital to our mission to be as accomodating as possible for all of your visistors.

History

First home to Esselen, Salinan, and Ohlone Native Americans, then taken by Spanish colonials, Big Sur is still largely comprised of wilderness. The Pacific Trails Resort was created to show visitors the beauty of this natural wilderness while preserving the history of the land. "Sur", meaning south, comes from the spanish colonials' naming of the area: "El Sur Grande". The land is protected now by many conservation laws as to preserve the beauty of the land.
