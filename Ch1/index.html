My favorite cafe is called Good Source. It is located in Escondido California. The drinks served under the welcoming atmosphere of this cafe are amazing. I have two go-to drinks. Their classic iced matcha latte is sweet amd full-bodied with a perfect ratio of matcha powder, sweetener, and oatmilk. Similarly, their iced salted maple latte is my favorite coffee I have ever had. Its sweet and slightly salty with a solid coffee flavor. All of the drinks they serve are perfectly creamy too!
